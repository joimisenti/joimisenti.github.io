About Me

I’m a software engineer with a background in biology and art pursuing a way to enmesh and enrich the best of both worlds. I am fascinated with the technical and the creative, function and form, mechanical and design.

As a recent Bright Paths full-stack coding bootcamp program graduate with Hackbright and DevMountain, I’m prepared to utilize my foundational skills and make them more realized. I have worked in academic research, medical and veterinary clinics, and biotechnology settings. The throughline of all these contexts hinged on support and collaboration, and I’m excited to bring this repertoire to sights unseen that are the next steps with my new people.

#.mood

Web App Development and Design

>>>Click Me to watch the Demo on YouTube

JavaScript | HTML | CSS | NodeJS | NPM | Axios | Sequelize | PostgreSQL

A mood tracker web application inspired by the customizability and personalization offered by Bullet Journals to empower Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) and mindfulness practices.

A capstone project to solidify and cement the foundations gained in my software engineering bootcamp, this app endeavored to be a cozy and delightful design that would evoke comfort and simplicity. The most discouraging aspect of any planner and mood template page set-up is the time and effort required, which this app was designed to do for you. As such, users can easily and quickly log their mood according to a list of colors and moods they craft to ultimately see a historical record for reflection of well-being. From observing the retrospect, patterns and trends extrapolate to debunk cognitive bias and enhance self-perception about the reality of your own mental health.
